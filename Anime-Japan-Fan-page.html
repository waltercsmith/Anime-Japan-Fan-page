A Brief Synopsis of Japan's History

This place is an island country but actually consists of a collection of islands (four large islands and several smaller ones). Active and dormant volcanoes are located in abundance and I had the honor of trekking up one... yes, that's right Mt. Fuji!! Yeah, I'm an adventurer at my core which only videogames and media only strengthen this notion. Japan contains a unique balance of upholding ancient traditions as well as a forerunner of technology and modernism. An example of this would be traveling to Kamakura to view the Great Buddha and a few block from this site is an heavy urbanized city with many technological commodities such as toilets that make sounds when you have a bout of flatulence. Population of Japan had an opportunity to develop their own unique identity due being isolated from larger landmasses such as China. The national language is Japanese but it's linguistic history vast and the rise of the Japanese language that is present today underwent a incredible transformation.
